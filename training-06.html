Programming

It answers two questions:

Von Neumann Architecture

Von Neumann Architecture

  • CPU: execute instructions
  • Memory: store data & intructions
  • I/O: take instruction to operate external devices
  • Bus: communicate among CPU, Memory, and I/O

Everything is accessed by address

Hello World! AdOps

console.log('Hello World!' + 'AdOps')

Memory Example

AddressMemory Content
001Get data from address 101, put it in Register A
002Get data from address 103, put it in Register B
003Concatenate data in register A and B, and put it in Register C
004Push data in Register C to address 102
. . .
101[String] Hello World!
102[Device] Screen Device
103[String] AdOps
. . .
